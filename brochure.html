Sample Itineraries

Day-by-Day Journey Plans

All itineraries are fully customisable. Below are the packages currently available from our portfolio — more destinations coming soon.

Uganda — The Pearl of Africa

Hiking (Mount Muhabura) + Safari Experience

5 Nights / 6 Days · Mix of wildlife safari and mountain hiking

Day 1

Tour of Kampala

Visit cultural sites and experience the local way of life in Uganda's vibrant capital.

Day 2

Travel to Lake Mburo National Park

Journey south with a stop at the Equator for photos and exploration.

Day 3

Morning Game Drive – Lake Mburo NP

Early morning game drive spotting zebras, impalas and buffalos, then travel to Kisoro in the south-west.

Day 4

Hiking Mount Muhabura

Hike this iconic volcano (3,645m) with interaction with local communities along the trail.

Note: Mountain may be swapped for Rwenzori or Elgon depending on preference.
Day 5

Return to Kampala

Scenic countryside views along the journey back with time for final shopping and reflection.

Quest for the Source of the Nile

5 Nights / 6 Days · Lake Victoria + Nile River Adventure

Day 1

Discover Islands on Lake Victoria

Boat out to the tranquil islands of Lake Victoria and interact with local fishing communities.

Day 2

Shoebill Stork Birding Experience

Rare birdwatching in the papyrus swamps to spot the iconic Shoebill Stork, plus kayaking on the lake.

Day 3

Visit the Source of the Nile

Visit the legendary source of the world's longest river in Jinja, with a boat cruise and sunset viewing.

Day 4

Visit the Busoga Kingdom

Cultural visit to the ancient Busoga Kingdom with bicycle riding and community interaction.

Day 5

White Water Rafting on the Nile

Thrilling grade 5 white water rafting on the Nile — the ultimate adrenaline finale before returning to Kampala.

Algeria — Sahara's Best Kept Secret

Desert Quest: Ghardaïa

5 Nights / 6 Days · Algiers to the Heart of the Sahara

Day 1

Arrival in Algiers

Arrival and hotel check-in. City tour including the iconic Casbah, Martyrs' Memorial, and the waterfront.

Day 2

Algiers Cultural Day

Visit museums, historic neighbourhoods, and local markets. Experience Algerian culture and cuisine.

Day 3

Algiers → Ghardaïa

Travel deep into the Sahara to Ghardaïa. Arrive, settle in, and enjoy an evening walk through the old town.

Day 4

M'zab Valley Exploration

Discover the UNESCO-listed M'zab Valley, its remarkable traditional architecture, markets, and cultural heritage sites.

Day 5

Return to Algiers

Free morning in Ghardaïa before returning to Algiers for departure.

Paths of Empires: Annaba to Constantine

5 Nights / 6 Days · Roman Heritage & Historic Cities

Day 1

Arrival in Annaba

Airport transfer and hotel check-in. Guided city tour of the region. Evening walk along the Corniche and waterfront.

Day 2

Roman Heritage of Hippo Regius

Discover the Roman ruins of Hippo Regius, the Basilica of Saint Augustine, and Cap de Garde for panoramic Mediterranean views.

Day 3

Annaba → Constantine

Travel to Constantine. City orientation tour and discovery of the historic old town and its cultural heritage.

Day 4

The Bridges of Constantine

Explore the famous bridges including Sidi M'Cid and Mellah Slimane. Visit the Emir Abdelkader Mosque, old medina, and traditional markets.

Day 5

Roman Sites & Panoramic Views

Cultural visit of Roman and historical sites, museums, and viewpoints overlooking Constantine's dramatic gorges. Free time for cafés and handicrafts.

Day 6

Constantine → Annaba & Departure

Return to Annaba. Free time before airport transfer and departure.

Benin — Cradle of African Spirituality

Sacred Benin: The Vodun Experience

5 Nights / 6 Days · Ancient Kingdoms + Spiritual Heritage

Day 1

Kingdom of Dahomey – Ancient Women Warriors

Visit the Amazons' historical sites, tour Africa's largest market, and receive a cultural orientation to this remarkable land.

Day 2

Temple of Pythons – Ouidah

Visit the ancient slave market, the Queen of Ouidah's palace, Sacred Forest, Sacred Garden, and the Fondation Zinsou.

Day 3

Vodun Dance Amphitheatre – Porte Nouvelle

International Museum of Vodoo, visit the Black River, learn about Spirit of Death traditions, and attend artisanal handicraft workshops.

Day 4

Floating Livelihood – Ganvié / Abomey-Calavi

Canoe ride through the extraordinary floating stilt village of Ganvié, then explore the new city and enjoy a picnic with locals.

Day 5

Day-to-Day Life of the Beninese People

Relaxing cultural activities and informal interactions with the warm and welcoming Beninese people.

📅 Best to travel between December and March — flights from Cadjehoun Airport (Cotonou) are generally more affordable during this period.
