Algeria – Sahara's Best Kept Secret

2 Packages Available

Algeria hides in plain sight — a nation vast as Western Europe yet unknown to most travellers. Here, endless golden dunes part to reveal Roman amphitheatres, Tuareg caravans trace routes older than recorded history, and the M'zab Valley stands frozen in medieval Islamic architecture. This is Africa's most underrated odyssey.

Benin – Cradle of African Spirituality

1 Package Available

Benin is where Africa whispers its oldest secrets. Once the heart of the mighty Dahomey kingdom — whose feared Agojie women warriors defied every empire — today this small nation holds more living history per square kilometre than almost anywhere on the continent. Voodoo is not a mystery here; it is Monday morning. Come to understand, and you will leave transformed.

Tanzania – Safari Capital of Africa

Safari Experience

Tanzania holds the most spectacular wildlife theatre on earth. The Serengeti's endless golden plains host the Great Migration — two million wildebeest, zebra and gazelle moving in an ancient, unstoppable tide. The Ngorongoro Crater contains more wildlife per square kilometre than anywhere on the planet. Tanzania does not merely offer a safari; it offers a reckoning with the wild.

🦁

Wildlife Safari Package

Our Tanzania offering focuses on expertly guided game drives through the country's iconic national parks and conservation areas. We curate each itinerary around the season and your interests — from the calving season on the short-grass plains to the dramatic river crossings of the Mara.

Kenya – The Original Safari Experience

Safari Experience

Kenya invented the word "safari" and still defines it. The Masai Mara is where every nature documentary has been filmed — where predator and prey engage in a drama unchanged since the Pleistocene. Beyond the game parks, the Maasai people offer a living cultural encounter, and the coast from Diani to Lamu is pure Indian Ocean paradise. Kenya delivers on every promise Africa makes.

🐘

Wildlife Safari Package

Kenya's safari circuit is among the world's most refined. Our guides are locally born and deeply knowledgeable, leading small groups through Big Five territory with emphasis on authentic, unhurried encounters. We handle all park fees, transfers, and lodging — you simply arrive and experience.

Rwanda – Land of a Thousand Hills

Safari Experience

Rwanda is Africa's phoenix — a nation reborn in green. Its mist-covered Virunga volcanoes shelter half the world's remaining mountain gorillas, and a morning spent in their quiet presence is a life-altering encounter. Rwanda is also Africa's cleanest, safest, and most efficiently run country. Coming here is not just a safari — it is a love letter to what Africa can be.

🦍

Primate & Wildlife Safari Package

Rwanda's signature experience is gorilla trekking in Volcanoes National Park — an intimate, hour-long audience with a mountain gorilla family that no photograph can fully prepare you for. We extend your journey with forest canopy walks, golden monkey tracking, and lakeside relaxation at Kivu.

Morocco – Where Africa Meets Europe

Safari Experience

Morocco is a sensory overload in the best possible way — cobalt-blue medina alleys, the rose-pink sands of the Erg Chebbi dunes at dusk, cedar forests roamed by wild Barbary macaques, and Atlas mountain villages where Berber hospitality is unchanged by centuries. No country on the continent blends desert, mountain, coast, and ancient city in such dizzying proximity.

🐪

Desert & Wildlife Safari Package

Our Morocco safari takes you beyond the medinas into wild Morocco — Barbary macaque encounters in the Middle Atlas, desert wildlife tracking in the Sahara, and high-altitude trekking in the Toubkal massif. We combine natural wonder with Morocco's legendary hospitality and cuisine for an experience of remarkable depth.
